The Best Movies According to Jenny

My Top 3 Movies All-Time

Lion King (1994)

The most beautiful disney-movie. I cry my eyes out everytime.

Inside Out (2015)

A genius and funny movie about feelings and psychology, explained in a educational way. Love the message and the way it was written.

Spider man (2002-)

Yes, this is sort of a movie series, but I love it. I like this type of hero that is not as "macho" in a sence. I like the ones with Tom Holland the best, then Tobey Maguire and lastly Andrew Garfield.
